Types of sarees:

Traditional sarees:

These sarees are the most popular type of Dharmavaram saree. They are typically made with a maroon or red border and a gold or silver pallu. The patterns on traditional Dharmavaram sarees are often inspired by temple murals and paintings. The most common motifs include elephants, peacocks, lotus flowers, and temple designs.

Jacquard sarees:

Jacquard sarees are characterized by their intricate and detailed patterns. The patterns are created using a jacquard loom, which allows for a wider range of designs than is possible with traditional weaving methods. Jacquard sarees are often more expensive than traditional Dharmavaram sarees, but they are also more elegant and eye-catching. Some popular jacquard patterns include paisleys, geometric shapes, and floral motifs.

Art silk sarees:

Art silk sarees are made with a blend of silk and art silk. Art silk is a synthetic fiber that is made to resemble silk. Art silk sarees are less expensive than pure silk sarees, but they are still very elegant and can be worn for special occasions. Some popular art silk patterns include floral motifs, paisleys, and geometric shapes.

Pattu sarees:

Pattu sarees are made with pure mulberry silk. Pattu silk is the highest quality type of silk and is known for its soft, luxurious feel. Pattu sarees are the most expensive type of Dharmavaram saree, but they are also the most prized. Some popular pattu patterns include intricate floral motifs, paisleys, and geometric shapes.

Floral sarees:

Floral sarees are a popular choice for weddings and other special occasions. They are typically woven with intricate floral patterns that can take months to complete. Some popular floral patterns include lotus flowers, roses, and jasmine.

Animal print sarees:

Animal print sarees are a more recent trend in Dharmavaram sarees. They are typically woven with patterns of animals such as elephants, tigers, and peacocks. Some popular animal print patterns include elephant motifs, tiger stripes, and peacock feathers.

Abstract sarees:

Abstract sarees are a great choice for those who want something unique and stylish. They are typically woven with intricate abstract patterns that are not inspired by any particular object or animal. Some popular abstract patterns include geometric shapes, swirls, and waves.
